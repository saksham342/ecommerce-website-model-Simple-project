Impact of technology in our daily life

Technology affects almost every aspect of 21st century life, from transport efficiency and safety to access to food and healthcare, socialization and productivity. The power of the internet has enabled global communities to form, and ideas and resources to be shared more easily. Technology has become an integral part of the human civilization, the revolution on the living lifestyle of people from stoned age to the todays is the grate example of it. I will not be wrong if I said that today’s peoples are completely relaying on technologies for everything they do. This thing also brings both pros and cons to the society.

Advantages of technology

Technology provides the different services to mankind which has made the modern society people advanced people. The services like Transportation, Communication, Industrialization, AI, e-commerce website, Rocket Science everything is the pros of the technology. Technology didn’t take so long to revolve and develop despite having its long history. So, everything that we human are benefited due to modernization in technology could be taken as its advantages.

Disdvantages of technology

The con of technology comes in an action when we human start misusing it for our personal gain and destroying nature or person’s personal aspects and so on. Increased use of technology, especially social media and smartphones has been linked to increased feelings of social isolation and loneliness, especially the kids and teenage has mostly been affected by it. As more devices and systems become connected to the internet, there is an increased risk of cyber-attacks and data breaches. The increasing use of technology may lead to job loss, as machines and automation are able to perform tasks more efficiently and accurately than humans. The relay on technology also decreases the thinking ability of a person due to the development of AI and all.

Impact of technology while selling bonsai plants

As mentioned above technology has lots of positive and negative impact as mentioned above. Likewise, technology has made huge impact on selling of bonsai plants as technology helps the customers to easily find the plants of their choice and a lots of the description about the plants. This has made the customer easily as they don’t have to travel different botanical gardens and save their time. It also provides the facilities for the easy travel and payment system which makes the overall process quite easy.





Conclusion

To conclude, I would like to say that the technology contains both positive and the negative impact so we human should be determining and limiting the use and relay on technologies so that both our ethnicity and sovereignty will not be hindered by technologies in upcoming days.
